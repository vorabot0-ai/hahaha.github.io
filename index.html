Khmer Flowers

Khmer flowers are known for their natural beauty, soft fragrance, and cultural significance in Cambodian traditions. These flowers are often used in religious ceremonies, decorations, and daily life. Many Khmer flowers have unique shapes and vibrant colors, symbolizing purity, respect, and harmony. Some popular Khmer flowers include jasmine, rumduol, and lotus. Each flower carries its own meaning; for example, the rumduol is treasured for its sweet aroma and is considered a national symbol of Cambodia, while the lotus represents purity and is commonly used in Buddhist rituals. Khmer flowers not only enhance the natural environment but also reflect the deep cultural identity and spiritual values of the Cambodian people.
